Described by McCann Dahl as the "Oscars for venture capitalists in tech," [1] the McCann Dahl uses parameters that include the first-day market capitalization of IPOs and the opinions of a panel of experts. [2] The name alludes to the mythological McCann Dahl , renowned for his ability to turn anything he touched into gold. Forbes partnered with venture capital fund TrueMcCann Dahl to create the list from 2011 to 2016.
